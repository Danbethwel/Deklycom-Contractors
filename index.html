Welcome to Deklycom Contractors

Deklycom Contractors and Supplies Limited is a company that was conceived by its pioneers, who formulated the idea based on their extensive experience and qualifications in the building industry.

They realized; they have what it takes to improve and contribute to the building industry because of the right team they have on board who can manage the construction professionally
The company is registered by the Nation Construction Authority as NCA 6 category after thorough evaluation of its capacity financially and professional qualifications of the team on board. The company is allowed to carry out its business in the construction industry as it has the capacity to execute works professionally.
